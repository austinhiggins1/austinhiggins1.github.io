Where do They Live?

Soon, These weirdly cool animals are going to die off if we are not careful! Nobody wants the Platypus to go extinct :(

It is important that we sustain the life of these animals because their numbers are diminishing. As of 2017, there is no platypus in captivity outside of Australia. Three attempts were made to bring the animals to the Bronx Zoo, in 1922, 1947, and 1958; of these, only two of the three animals introduced in 1947 lived longer than eighteen months.
